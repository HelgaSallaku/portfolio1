Let me introduce < / >

My name is Helga Sallaku. I am currently working as Director of IT Department at American Dental Companies LLC, based in Phoenix. My origin is from Albania, Eastern Europe, and I have moved here in Arizona in the recent last year. Regarding my expertise I can confirm to you almost 19 years of working experience in the field of Information Communication and Technology (ICT) contributing in its development in the city of Shkodra (the city where I come from) and beyond it, around Albania (my home country). I am graduated in Informatics, holding first the bachelor degree at the University of Shkodra "Luigj Gurakuqi" and later I owned the title of Master of Science in "Information Systems - Informatics", completed at the Faculty of Natural Sciences, University of Tirana. I am currently a PhD Candidate ate the University of Montenegro and my field of research is related to "Sustainable Development" with focus in Iot and AI integrated Applications. In my home country I have been working as Computer Science Lecturer in the Department of Mathematics, Session of Informatics, in the Faculty of Natural Sciences at the University of Shkodra "Luigj Gurakuqi", having a long experience in the field of teaching at the university level since 2008 and in designing and managing professional study programs at the pre-university and university level, within the assessment methods with the integration and support of digital tools. I have a long experience in mentorship of the teachers as a mentor at national level, fostering them to the usage of the innovative methodologies as Critical Thinking and Problem Solving (21 Century Skillls) techniques in teaching and learning process. For 13 years, I have been the head of the ICT sector in the Municipality of Shkodër, a period which has contributed in my professional growth expertise in local government level and e-government procedures systems automation. My intellectual and professional training is followed by dozens of certificates, diplomas and awards in the field of informatics, teaching in IT and in STEM Education, being added recently in the past two years the Scientix Ambassador title, awarded form the academic network of the European SchoolNet Academy, as one of 8 representatives of Albania. At the same time, I am a passionate innovative young entrepreneur woman engaged in the development of two successful startups in Shkodër, in the field of professional training & mentoring and in software development. I am the founder of the Vocational Training Centre based in Shkodra City called “GLOBE ICT & Language Education”, which delivers innovative courses for children and adults related to: STEM education, coding skills, IT, foreign languages, social sciences, financial field, etc.; and a couple of years later I supported the implementation of the first Software House based in Shkodra City, an innovative initiative for the software development delivery in outsourcing through the motto: From Shkodra to the World. During 2022-2023 I have been a team member at the University of Arizona Center for Innovation, as an Innovation Researcher, supporting them with science and technology companies at all stages of their development in Southern Arizona. During that period of time, I had the chance to be part of Women in STEM Mentorship Program as a Mentor, as a Mentor of Computer Science bachelor students of the University of Arizona.
